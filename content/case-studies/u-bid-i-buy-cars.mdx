## Project Overview

U-BID-I-BUY-CARS aimed to revolutionize the online car auction market by providing a transparent and efficient platform for both individual sellers and professional dealers.

## The Solution

The platform was developed using **Django** for its powerful ORM and admin capabilities, while **Fast API** handled the real-time bidding engine to ensure minimal latency during auctions.

### Key Features

- **Real-Time Bidding:** A low-latency auction engine for live car bidding.
- **Inventory Management:** Comprehensive tools for sellers to list and manage their vehicles.
- **Vehicle History Integration:** Automated retrieval of car history reports.
- **Advanced Search:** Multi-criteria filtering for buyers to find the perfect vehicle.

## Technologies Used

- **Django:** Backend framework and inventory management.
- **Fast API:** Real-time auction engine.
- **WebSockets:** For live bidding updates.
- **Redis:** For high-speed caching and session management.

## Results

- **Significant Increase** in auction participation.
- **Streamlined Process** for car listing and verification.
- **Robust Performance** during peak auction times.
